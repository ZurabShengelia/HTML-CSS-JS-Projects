WHAT WE TEST FOR

Purity Testing

Minimum 99.8% purity standard across all products. HPLC and mass spectrometry analysis confirms active ingredient concentrations match label claims exactly.

Potency Verification

Every ingredient is tested to confirm it delivers on its label claim. Underdosed products don't reach our shelves — full stop.

Heavy Metal Analysis

All products screened for lead, arsenic, cadmium, and mercury. Our standards exceed EPA safety levels by a significant margin.

Microbial Screening

Comprehensive testing for bacteria, yeast, and mold. Every batch must pass before leaving our facility.

Banned Substance Testing

Screened against WADA and USADA prohibited substance lists. Safe for competitive athletes at any level.

Certificate of Analysis

Every batch gets a CoA you can verify. Athlete transparency is non-negotiable — scan any QR code on our products to view results.
